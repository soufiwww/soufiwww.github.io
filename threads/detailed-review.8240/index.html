I bought my first sub when the v4 got released, but I have used the v3 crack as well

RAGEBOT:
-resolver: It's hard to hit head with scout and sometimes I miss stomach due to resolver (0.73 head scale, 0.59 body scale)-the resolver needs an update [6/10]
-prediction: The prediction is good for a 5$ cheat. [9/10]
-exploits: The DT is fast and accurate and you can ideal tick with scout. The "hide shots" is working correctly. [9/10]
-overall rating: 8/10

ANTI-AIM:
-It is not as customizable as it used to be in the v3 version.
-You can improve the AA with scripts (like iugenScript, SeaSide etc.)
-rating: 6/10

LEGITBOT:
-The legitbot is decent but it's not perfect.
-You can't go under 1 FOV.
-It's much better than it used to be in the v3 version.
-rating: 7/10

VISUALS:
-I like the visuals. You can customize the ESP as you want.
-rating: 9/10

MISC:
-You can find anything you need in a rage cheat.
-rating: 10/10

LUA API:
-The API is not bad itself, but you can get weird errors with no reason.
-rating: 6/10

OVERALL CHEAT RATING: 7.5/10

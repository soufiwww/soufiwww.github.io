You have many luas free and others paid.
What is the difference between them?
The free lua you have bad antiaim/ resolver, and maybe some luas are broken and you don’t receive any update.
In paid luas like SunRise.lua, Seaside, iugenScript, etc … you normally have frequent updates with many top functions and the functions are very good. The antiaim is better than normal lwv4 and the resolver can hit more than free luas and lwv4 resolver. DT can shoot more fast, you can have jumpscout and other things in only one lua.
I've summed it up a lot to make it simple to understand.
